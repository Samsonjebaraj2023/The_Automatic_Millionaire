## The Latte Factor Challenge

### Track Your Spending for One Day

**Purpose:** Discover your personal Latte Factor

**How to Do It:**
1. Write down EVERY purchase for 24 hours
2. Include all expenses (no matter how small)
3. Identify habitual, unnecessary spending
4. Calculate daily/monthly/yearly totals
5. Imagine that money invested instead

**Outcome:** Awareness leads to intentional change
